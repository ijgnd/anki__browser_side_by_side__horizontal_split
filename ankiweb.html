This add-on allows to move the editor from the bottom of to the right side of the table. You can configure the shortcut and if it's side-by-side by default. Use this at your own risk! # known limitations, problems, incompatibilities - Browser Maximize/Hide Table/Editor: They mostly work together but the table and or editor is not fully hidden. - Card Info Bar for Browser: They mostly work together but you have to close and re-open the info bar to adjust its width. # bugs Please do not use reviews for bug reports or support requests.
